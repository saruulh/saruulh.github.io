Cultural Blog: Pairing and Giving Feedback

March 29, 2015

Pairing

I thought it would be hard to pair with others, but I actually found it a very rewarding experience. Not only did it allow me to get my thoughts out of my own head, but it also allowed me to share what I was going through with others who were in the same boat as me. I believe it made the long days and nights a bit more enjoyable.

In addition, working with others to solve a problem allows me to think of different ways to attack the problem. By pairing with different cohorts each time I get to experience and hear different perspectives on the subject.

I think it will help in the future working with a team of people to achieve a common goal.

When I am going through a challenge by myself, there is nobody there to have a mini celebration with. While my husband gives me encouragement, he really has no idea what I’m talking about because he isn’t sharing the same experience. I think the cohorts and I are experiencing the same frustration and joy together, so when we solve a problem on a pairing session, it’s rewarding.

Overall, I’m happy with how the pairings go, though there were a couple of times where my pair seemed distracted by something else or when we both stuck on something. Still, it was a good experience because dealing with frustrating situations and overcoming them is what life is all about.

Feedback

I was thrilled to see that people felt I was patient and that they had a good time working pairing with me. I’m working pretty hard to stay up with all the reading and exercises, so it’s nice to hear that others appreciate my levels of knowledge. I want people to be patient with me, so the fact that they feel I treat them the same is wonderful.

It was helpful to see where my strong and weak points are, though I feel the feedback from the instructors are more helpful. I think, as fellow cohorts, we are afraid to hurt each other’s feelings. But constructive criticism is always welcome!

I think I will continue to stay patient with my pairs because that’s what we all need at this point. I find that writing a feedback is somewhat hard for me though, personally, for the simple reason that I'm a fellow student and I'm standing at the same side of the fence. I realize we are all in this together, but I try my best.
