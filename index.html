Cultural Blog: Questions

April 26, 2015

Throughout this whole process, Google has helped me tremendously. I found that most of my questions were already answered as I researched what I was looking for. However, as in life, there were times when I exhausted all resources and need to seek for help. Therefore, asking the right question the could help both the person who's asking and the person who's answering.

I think the best way going about asking the right question the right way is to think about exactly what you need to find out and how you would phrase a concise question without being too vague. When you have your question phrased ask yourself that question and see if it makes sense to you. Look for imporovements to make it even easier to understand for others and of course, check your spelling.

I did a bit of homework previously on how to ask the right questions when I worked with my clients. I used to read books and listened webinars on this subject. I hopeful that this will carry over to my time in DevBootcamp.
